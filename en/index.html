Why this helps (without drama)

Respect

You share fewer extra identifiers others never asked for.

Cleaner communication

Links look clearer and less ad-like.

Less breakage risk

Some apps truncate or rewrite very long URLs poorly.

Local-first

No need to paste links into random third-party cleaners.

Do not remove everything blindly

Affiliate / partner attribution

Some parameters are intentional attribution (e.g., ref, tag). Removing them can break payout/credit.

Pro: allowlists and per-domain policies.

Functional parameters

Some keys control content (language, filter, product ID, search state). Good cleaners stay conservative.

App: standard tracking keys. Pro: audit + rules.
