Why this helps (without drama)

Respect

You share less extra data other people didn’t choose.

More credible

Links look cleaner and less “ad-like”.

Less breakage

Some apps shorten/modify long URLs in unhelpful ways.

Local-first

Don’t blindly remove everything

Affiliate/partner

Some parameters are attribution (e.g. ref, tag). If you remove them, compensation/attribution may break.

Pro: allowlists/policies per domain.

Functional parameters

Some keys control content (language, filters, product ID, search). Good tools are conservative and don’t remove things blindly.

App: standard tracking. Pro: audit + rules.
