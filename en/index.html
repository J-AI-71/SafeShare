Remove tracking add-ons. Share clean links.

Links often carry extra label-like data (utm_*, fbclid, gclid …).

Useful for analytics in some cases, often just noise when forwarding. SafeShare removes common tracking keys locally in your browser.

100% local-first. No sign-up. No tracking.

SafeShare is local-first: cleaning happens in your browser. Links are not uploaded.
This is not an “invisible online” promise. It is a concrete hygiene step before sharing.
