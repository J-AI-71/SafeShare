FAQ

Is Pro a subscription?

No. Pro is not a subscription. SafeShare stays free to use.

What does “local-first” mean?

Your link is cleaned in your browser. You’re not sending it to a server that processes it “for you”.

Does SafeShare promise anonymity or invisibility?

No. SafeShare removes tracking parameters and link ballast. That’s link hygiene — not an invisibility promise.
