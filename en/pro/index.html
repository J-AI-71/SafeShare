Frequently asked questions about SafeShare Pro

Who is the Supporter plan (€9) for?

The Supporter plan is for individuals with occasional usage who want clean links and want to support SafeShare.

When does Pro Person (€49) pay off?

Pro Person pays off for recurring link hygiene. It is especially useful for solo operators, creators, consultants, and operational roles with regular link workflows.

When does Pro Team (€249) pay off?

Pro Team pays off when multiple people need shared standards. It supports repeatable workflows, lower coordination overhead, and stronger auditability.

What is the key difference between Pro Person and Pro Team?

Pro Person is designed for one person’s workflow. Pro Team is designed for shared standards, team-wide consistency, and governance/audit needs.

Are links uploaded in SafeShare Pro?

No. SafeShare Pro is used as an offline download. The workflow is local-first and does not require link uploads in the app flow.
