Who is Pro for?

Publishing / Journalism / Social team

  • You publish lots of links and want them to look clean.
  • You want standard tracking removed without breaking functional parameters.
  • You need a clear, repeatable process.

Affiliate / Partners / Growth

  • You must intentionally keep attribution (e.g., ref, tag).
  • You want domain rules: what stays, what goes.
  • You want to see exactly what changed.
Pro is not an “anonymity tool.” It’s link hygiene: less ballast in the URL — clearer, more respectful, more transparent.

What’s different in Pro vs. the App?

FAQ (quick)

Why not just remove everything?

Because some parameters are functional or attribution. Pro is built to separate “remove” and “keep” cleanly.

What if a link breaks after cleaning?

Then a key was likely functional. Pro helps: check the audit → allow the key → clean again.
