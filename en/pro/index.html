Why SafeShare Pro costs money

You can use SafeShare for free. Link cleaning is not a lead magnet here and not an “email-for-freebie” trick. It is the core: local-first in your browser, no account, no data collection.

What is free – and stays free

  • Clean links (remove tracking parameters, create clean URLs)
  • Local-first: runs in your browser, no server relay
  • No account, no login, no forced subscription
  • No fear-based UI, no artificial limits, no dark patterns

Privacy is not a premium add-on

Many tools sell “privacy” as a paid feature. Here, privacy is the foundation – not the upgrade. Pro does not mean “more privacy for money”, but more clarity and a better workflow for people who use this regularly.

What you actually pay for with Pro

You pay for real work and long-term reliability – not pressure or psychological tricks. Pro helps fund:

  • Ongoing development (new parameters, new redirect patterns, platform-specific cases)
  • Maintenance (browser updates, iOS/Safari quirks, regression fixes)
  • Quality & stability (clean releases, testing, transparent changes)
  • Documentation (handbook, explanations, examples)
  • Offline / Pro workflows (convenience features that take real effort to build)

Who SafeShare Pro is for

FAQ

Is Pro a subscription?

No. Pro is intended as a one-time purchase (depending on the package). SafeShare itself remains free to use.

What does “local-first” mean in practice?

Your link is cleaned in your browser. You are not sending your links to a server that processes them for you.

Does SafeShare promise anonymity or invisibility?

No. SafeShare removes tracking parameters and link ballast. That is link hygiene — not an invisibility promise.

How do updates work?

Pro helps fund ongoing maintenance and development. For update windows/versions, see the individual product descriptions.
