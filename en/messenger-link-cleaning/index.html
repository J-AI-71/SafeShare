Quick Workflows

Copy → Clean → Share (single link)

  1. Copy the link from app/browser.
  2. Open SafeShare → paste → clean.
  3. Share the cleaned link in messenger.

Multiple links for team/community (Pro)

  1. Collect multiple URLs (post, note, sheet).
  2. Review with Pro policy/audit.
  3. Post consistently cleaned links in chat/channel.

Important limit (honest)

Don’t remove everything blindly: parameters (e.g. ref, tag) may be intentionally used for attribution.

Use Pro rules when you need domain-level control.

Why this helps in daily messaging

Less data spread

In group forwards, links spread quickly. Clean URLs reduce unnecessary metadata across that chain.

Clearer communication

Shorter cleaned URLs look more professional and are easier to read, review, and document.
