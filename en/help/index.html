FAQ

Do you upload links?

No. Cleaning runs local-first in your browser.

Why does a parameter sometimes stay?

Some parameters are functional (e.g., language, product ID). SafeShare does not remove everything blindly.

Can I keep affiliate/ref parameters?

Yes. Use Pro rules/allowlists per domain for fine control.

Pro does not start?

Unzip the package fully and start with 01-START.html.
