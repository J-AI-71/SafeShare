What SafeShare is — and what it is not

What SafeShare does

  • Removes tracking parameters from URLs (e.g. utm_*, fbclid, gclid) before sharing.
  • Reduces unnecessary data traces in the link.
  • Runs locally in your browser (no link upload to SafeShare servers).

What SafeShare does not promise

  • Being invisible on the web does not exist — never.
  • No protection against destination-site IP logs.
  • No protection against fingerprinting or post-login tracking.
  • Not a digital ID and not a wallet.

Short version: SafeShare is not a myth. It is a concrete reduction step: clean the link, then share.

Quick Help

Link is not recognized

  1. Paste the full URL (https://…).
  2. Remove spaces before/after the link.
  3. If copied from messages, copy only the link.

Result stays unchanged

  1. Check whether the link has parameters (?).
  2. Reload the page and try again.
  3. If needed, test another browser (Safari/Firefox/Chrome).

Page does not load correctly

  1. Reload with cache refresh.
  2. Temporarily disable browser extensions.
  3. Test again afterwards.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does SafeShare remove?

SafeShare removes common tracking parameters such as utm_*, gclid, or fbclid. The goal is a clean link without unnecessary tracking extras.

Why do some parameters stay in the link?

Not every parameter is tracking. Some values are technically required (e.g. language, page, product ID), so they remain to keep the link functional.

Are my links stored or uploaded?

No. Cleanup runs locally in your browser. Input links are not stored on a server.

Can I use SafeShare for messenger, email, and social media?

Yes. You can use cleaned links in WhatsApp, Signal, email, newsletters, and social posts.

Does SafeShare work on mobile?

Yes. SafeShare works on smartphone, tablet, and desktop directly in the browser.

FAQ: Digital ID, wallets, and SafeShare

Does SafeShare make me invisible online?

No. Being invisible on the web is not possible. SafeShare reduces one specific part of your trace: tracking extras in shared URLs.

Is SafeShare a digital ID or a wallet?

No. SafeShare is neither a digital ID nor a wallet. SafeShare cleans links before sharing.

Why does link cleaning still matter?

Because many extra parameters are not needed for content delivery but are used for measurement and attribution. SafeShare removes this surplus.
