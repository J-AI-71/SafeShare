FAQ

Does the shortcut store links?

No. Processing is local on your device.

Why two shortcuts?

To keep “Standard” truly one tap and reserve “Mode select” for special cases.

Is shortcut enough instead of Pro?

For quick sharing, yes. For rules/audit/bulk workflows, use Pro.
