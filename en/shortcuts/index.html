FAQ

Does the shortcut store my links or usage data?

No. It runs locally on your device.

Why two shortcuts?

To keep Standard truly one tap. Mode switch is for special cases.

What does “Strict” do?

It removes more tracking/referrer-like keys. If something breaks, use Standard.

Why not use a URL shortener?

Shorteners hide URLs. SafeShare removes tracking noise from the URL itself.

I see a security warning when adding a shortcut. Is that normal?

Yes. iOS warns about imported automations. Review before adding.

I need audit/policies/bulk cleanup. Is the shortcut enough?

Shortcut is great for quick sharing. For audit/policies/bulk/domain rules, use SafeShare Pro.
