What to watch out for

Do not remove everything blindly: some parameters are functional or intentionally used for attribution.
  • Affiliate/ref: keys like ref or tag may be intentional.
  • Functional: language, product IDs, filters, and search states.
  • Pro: use per-domain rules/allowlists for precise control.
