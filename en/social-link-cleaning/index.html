Social Link Cleaning

Share cleaner links instead of passing tracking noise.

Social URLs often include campaign parameters and click IDs. In posts, profiles, and comments, these are frequently unnecessary. SafeShare removes common tracking parameters local-first.

Limits to keep in mind

Link cleaning is a hygiene step, not an invisibility promise.
  • Attribution: some campaign parameters are intentionally set.
  • Functionality: certain parameters may control content state.
  • Pro: use domain-level rules to keep only what you need.
