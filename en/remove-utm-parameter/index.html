Remove UTM parameters

Share cleaner links with less noise.

UTM parameters are added to URLs for campaign measurement. Useful for analytics, often unnecessary when forwarding links to people.

What are UTM parameters?

?utm_source=...&utm_medium=...&utm_campaign=...

utm_source

Traffic source (e.g. newsletter, linkedin, mastodon)

utm_medium

Channel type (e.g. email, social, cpc)

utm_campaign

Campaign label (e.g. launch_q1)

Why remove them?

Cleaner URLs

Shorter, clearer links in chats, emails, docs.

Data minimization

Less extra metadata attached when you share.

Fewer formatting issues

Very long query strings break less often.

Consistent workflow

Copy → Clean → Share as a simple habit.

Do not remove blindly

Not every parameter is pure tracking. Some keys are functional, others intentionally used for referral attribution.

SafeShare app removes standard tracking conservatively. For fine control, use SafeShare Pro (rules/allowlist/audit).
