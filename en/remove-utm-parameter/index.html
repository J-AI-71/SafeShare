Remove UTM Parameters

Understand campaign markers. Share intentionally.

UTM parameters (utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign …) are useful for marketing analytics. When forwarding links, they are often unnecessary.

What do UTM fields mean?

utm_source

Traffic source, e.g. newsletter, linkedin, mastodon.

utm_medium

Channel type, e.g. email, social, cpc.

utm_campaign

Campaign name, e.g. spring_launch.

utm_term / utm_content

Optional: keywords and creative variants.
