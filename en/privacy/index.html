Privacy

Clear and minimal.

1. Core principle

SafeShare is local-first. Link cleaning runs in your browser on your device. Links are not uploaded to a SafeShare server.

2. Data processed

URLs you paste are processed locally for cleaning. No mandatory account is required for standard use.

3. Purchases

Payments/orders for Pro/Supporter are handled by the checkout provider (e.g., Payhip), under their policies.

4. Hosting logs

Technical connection data may be processed by hosting/CDN providers (e.g., IP address, timestamp, user agent).
